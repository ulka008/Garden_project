Welcome to our Herb Garden

Our herb garden was created during the 2020 pandemic, when the world changed and the children needed to be kept busy.

We love to cook, so we decided to create a small herb garden.

The hardest part was to choose the herbs we want to plant. Since we had limited space, we decided to go with the ones we love and use the most.

Thanks to lots of watering and sunshine, we are impatiently waiting to harvest our herbs in just a little bit.

Supplies

Supplies needed are easily found around the house or bought in the stores.

Here are just some of the examples of the containers we used:

  • A plastic container from sour cream.
  • A plastic container from yougurt.
  • A cut milk jug.
  • A clear plastic container from ready-to-eat salad.
  • A metal can from canned vegetables or fruits.
  • A plastic bucket.
  • A flower pot.

You will also need seeds of your choice and garden soil.


Saw your seeds, water the soil and put your container in a sunny spot.

Don't forget to water your future herbs!

Have fun!
